Mission

Our Mission

Our mission is to support men in the quest for lives of compassion, integrity, responsibility, and balance.


Through mutually supportive work, our fellowship seeks to help men in their development as sons... Fathers... Brothers... Companions and members of society.


We are a caring, intentionally diverse brotherhood, committed to social justice and offering opportunities for intimate sharing, personal growth, and spiritual deepening.


You don't need to be a member of First UU San Diego, and you don't need to identify with any religion. You just need to be a man who wants deeper community.

How the Fellowship Works

The fellowship operates through three layers of community, each offering a different depth of engagement.

🌙

Fourth Mondays

Our main monthly gathering. Potluck dinner at 6pm followed by check-in and discussion from 7–9pm. Open to all men. Most meetings available on Zoom.

🏕️

Renewal Weekends

Three-day mountain retreats each Fall and Spring. Life-changing events for many men — newcomers especially welcome. Fall in Julian, Spring in the mountains east of LA.

Focus Groups

Small groups of five to eight men that meet regularly — at members' homes or on Zoom. Built on trust, confidentiality, and long-term commitment.

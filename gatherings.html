Every Month

Fourth Mondays

Our primary monthly gathering. The evening begins with a shared potluck dinner and ends with meaningful conversation among brothers.

The Evening

6pm
Potluck Dinner

Bring a dish to share — or just come as you are if it's your first time. No dish required for newcomers. We gather, share food, and connect informally.

7pm
Circle Check-In

We open with a check-in round — each man shares briefly how he's doing, what's alive for him. No cross-talk, no advice. Just being heard.

7:30
Discussion

A topic or theme relevant to men's lives — chosen by the facilitator or the group. Past topics include fatherhood, grief, friendship, identity, purpose, and more.

9pm
Closing

We close with a chant or song as a group. The circle ends, and men linger to connect informally.

What to Know

Where

Common Room, Hillcrest Campus
First Unitarian Universalist Church of San Diego

Zoom

Most meetings are available on Zoom. Contact a co-convener for the link — see Connect page.

First Time?

Just show up. You don't need to bring anything. You don't need to be a church member. Arrive by 6pm for dinner, or come at 7pm for the discussion. You'll be welcomed.

Is it Religious?

No religious requirement. UUMF draws from UU principles — inclusive, non-creedal, spiritually open. Men of any background are welcome.

Special Occasions

Annual Celebrations

Two special gatherings each year mark the depth of fellowship built through the months.

🍽️

February Banquet

Our annual catered banquet held each February — a celebration of the fellowship and the men in it. A highlight of the year and a wonderful event to bring someone new. Dress well, eat well, be together.

The banquet replaces the regular Fourth Monday meeting in February.

📅 Each February · Date announced at January Fourth Monday
🪵

Yule Log Ceremony

A beloved seasonal tradition during the holiday season. The Yule Log ceremony is a time of reflection, ritual, and brotherhood as the year draws to a close. Men are invited to bring something symbolic to release into the fire — a word, a worry, an old story.

Typically held at the December Fourth Monday.

🕯️ Holiday Season · Details announced at November Fourth Monday
